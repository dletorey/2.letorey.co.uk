Last night I went to see Yard Act at Hammersmith Apollo. This was the second time this year seeing them, the first was at Pryzm Kington for their Album launch of Where's my Utopia.

Last nights gig was brilliant and I'd rate it 5/5. The band were on top form and the crowd were lapping up everything thrown at them.

During the tour each night one track in the set list was decided by a member of the audience spinning a wheel, last night it was the turn of Tara who's birthday it was and here friends were on stage with her and presented her a colin the caterpillar cake. After a rousing rendition of Happy birthday Tarra span the wheel and Peanuts was chosen.

If you get a chance to see them this year then just at it, the put on a great show.
