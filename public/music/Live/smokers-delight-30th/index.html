This was just a pure celebration of great music.

You could see this as everyone in the crowd was happy and dancing along. The range of ages in the crowd was huge, I was really not expecting this. It just goes to show how universal these two albums really are.

At the end of the gig, George brought all of his family and friends onto the stage and shouted out to everyone who had ever been apart of his life.

If you have never come across Nightmares on Wax, then you should really check out these two albums:

  • Smokers Delight
  • In a Space Outta Sound
